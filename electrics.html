Consulting Overview

Epie Electricians has evolved from hands-on electrical maintenance into a consultancy for solar PV design and low-voltage technology integration. Our expertise now spans large-scale energy planning, technical problem-solving, and software development.

Solar Solutions

We design and consult on custom solar PV systems, helping clients across Africa transition to sustainable energy. Our work includes feasibility analysis, layout planning, and supervision of local installations.

Software & Systems

Through our background in extra-low voltage systems, we've moved into the world of software development—creating tools that blend electrical logic with intuitive design. Expect more engineering-focused apps in the future.

Past Work

We’ve provided electrical solutions for homes and businesses, and now we offer planning and supervision for engineering teams carrying out larger scale work.
