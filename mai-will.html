ImmortalAI – The Origin

ImmortalAI began as a philosophical exploration—an idea that artificial intelligence could preserve our truths, intentions, and legacies beyond our lifetimes. That vision gave birth to the MAI Will app.

What is MAI Will?

MAI Will is an intelligent system designed to help individuals tag and preserve their personal legacies. It uses AI to record, timestamp, and protect intentions—creating digital proof that lives on without human intervention.
